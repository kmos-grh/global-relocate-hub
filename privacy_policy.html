1. Overview

Global Relocate Hub (GRH) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our relocation platform and services.

By using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to transparency and will notify you of any changes to this policy.

2. Data Collection

Contact Forms

When you fill out contact forms on our website, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number (when provided)
  • Inquiry details and messages
  • Relocation preferences and requirements

Notify Me Modals

When you sign up for notifications, we collect:

  • Email address for updates
  • Notification preferences
  • Selected routes or services of interest

Partner Requests

When requesting partner access or services, we collect:

  • Company information and contact details
  • Service requirements and specifications
  • Business verification information
  • Communication history and preferences

Newsletter Signups

For newsletter subscriptions, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Content preferences
  • Subscription date and status

3. Cookie Usage

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website usage.

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality, security, and user preferences. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand user behavior and improve our services. You can opt-out through cookie preferences.

Marketing Cookies

Used for personalized content and advertising. Fully optional and controlled by your preferences.

Your Cookie Controls

You have full control over cookie preferences through:

  • Our cookie banner on first visit
  • Cookie settings modal (accessible anytime)
  • Floating cookie settings button
  • Browser settings for additional control

4. GDPR Compliance

As a platform serving users globally, including the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar privacy laws worldwide.

Your GDPR Rights Include:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Update or correct inaccurate personal information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional cookies
  • Legitimate Interest: For providing our services and improving user experience
  • Contractual Necessity: For partner agreements and service delivery

5. Lead Generation

Partner Referrals

When you request partner services through our platform:

  • We share relevant contact information with verified partners
  • Partners may contact you directly regarding your inquiry
  • You can opt-out of partner communications at any time
  • We monitor partner interactions to ensure quality service

Data Sharing Practices

We only share data with:

  • Verified partners in our network for legitimate service provision
  • Third-party service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Analytics Data

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand user behavior and improve our services:

  • Page views and user sessions
  • Traffic sources and referrals
  • Geographic information (country/region level)
  • Device and browser information

Data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt-out through cookie preferences or Google's opt-out tools.

User Behavior Tracking

We track anonymized user interactions to improve our platform:

  • Tool usage and calculator interactions
  • Content engagement and time spent
  • Form completion rates and user journeys
  • Error tracking for technical improvements
